----About My Project----

It's a TIC-TAC-TOE game which is very popular among beginner level JS problem. Basically there are 3 rows and 3 columns in this game, and there are two players who can play against. One player can submit X and other one can submit O in that particular 3 columns and rows. After that if any of the player's submitted value X or O is perfectly aligned (Straight or criss-cross) then the user will win.
